# How To Buy The $99 Workflow Audit

This is the buying path for the 24-hour AI Workflow Audit + One Automation Fix.

## 1. Check Fit

The starter offer is for one repeated workflow with a clear input and desired output.

Good fits:

- Campaign notes to client-ready recap bullets.
- Lead notes to CRM-ready rows.
- Intake details to scoped work orders.
- Spreadsheet cleanup into a repeatable CSV template.
- A small prompt workflow, checklist, SOP, or lightweight script for a weekly task.

Not a fit:

- Full product builds.
- Guaranteed sales or revenue outcomes.
- Spam, scraping against site rules, account evasion, or regulated decisioning.
- Work requiring production secrets before a secure process is agreed.
