# Weekly Reporting Automation Consultant For Marketing Agencies

A $99 starter offer for agencies and marketing operations teams that spend too much time turning weekly notes, exports, and status updates into client-ready reporting.

I turn one repeated weekly reporting workflow into a reusable prompt, checklist, recap template, CSV cleanup step, SOP, handoff note, or lightweight script.

## Best Fit

- Account managers rewrite campaign notes into weekly client updates.
- Paid media, SEO, content, or B2B teams clean spreadsheet exports before reporting.
- Client status updates need the same structure every week.
- Meeting notes need to become client-ready recap bullets and next actions.
- Reporting handoffs need a safer path from raw notes to polished commentary.

- A short workflow intake.

- Reuse notes so the team can run the weekly reporting workflow again.
- One revision if the delivered fix does not match the agreed safe sample and output.
