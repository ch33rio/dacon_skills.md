# CRM Cleanup Automation Consultant For Marketing Teams

A $99 starter offer for marketing operations, agencies, and B2B teams that repeat CRM cleanup, lead handoff, or HubSpot-style reporting work every week.

I turn one recurring CRM or lead-data workflow into a reusable prompt, checklist, CSV cleanup step, SOP, handoff note, or lightweight script.

## Best Fit

- Lead notes need to become CRM-ready rows.
- B2B signal notes need consistent sales handoff fields.
- HubSpot, Salesforce, or spreadsheet exports need repeatable cleanup before review.
- Account managers need client or pipeline notes cleaned into consistent next actions.
- Weekly reporting needs CRM context summarized without exposing private customer data.

## What You Get

- A short workflow intake.
- A map of the current CRM cleanup workflow, bottleneck, and useful fix.
- One lightweight improvement: prompt workflow, checklist, CSV cleanup step, SOP, CRM handoff note, or small script.
- Reuse notes so the team can run the CRM cleanup workflow again.
- One revision if the delivered fix does not match the agreed safe sample and output.
